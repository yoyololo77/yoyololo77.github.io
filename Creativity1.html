Being Creative or Being Normal

By Yoanna Checo

This is about a girl who is having a hard time choosing what she wants to do with her life. She doesnt know whether to choose the path of creativity or to choose the path to be normal without the creativity.

The girl finally chooses which path she should take and she choose creativity as her path.
